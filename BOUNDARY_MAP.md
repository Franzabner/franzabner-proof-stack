## Held Repo Rule

Risky rack, agent, Fusion, KiCad production, production RIB, infrastructure repos, and real-case analysis lanes remain held until a human-approved boundary review scopes their public updates or derivative-only replacement. `local-ai-lab-boundary` is live as a public boundary model, `dgx-ai-workstation-notes` is live as public DGX/NVIDIA workstation workflow documentation, `field-propulsion-observatory` is live as public-safe evidence-boundary simulation documentation, and `s2h-g-control-ledger` is live as a public proof surface for proprietary simulation-first energy-control documentation. `fine-tuning-lab-notes`, `hf-card-templates`, `energy-per-intelligence`, and `epi-bench` are archived-legacy candidates only after Batch 6 migration. None of these repos makes the real studio, lab, DGX, agent, fine-tuning, model, dataset, infrastructure implementation, real anomalous-motion case conclusion, propulsion claim, or private S2H-G implementation public.

## Refusal Rule

Do not publish, summarize outward, or convert an item into proof when its evidence depends on private implementation, sealed source, customer or employer material, credentials, private infrastructure, unreleased models, unreleased datasets, production CAD/KiCad/USD assets, or Foundation private operations.
